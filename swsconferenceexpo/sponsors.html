National Gunite - Booth 205
National Gunite provides trenchless repair of sewers and storm culverts using concrete that is sprayed onto steel reinforcement. Over time, concrete, brick and metal lines deteriorate, which causes leaks and cave-ins. These lines can be repaired from inside the pipe by lining the pipe with steel mesh or rebar and spraying the surface with multiple inches of Gunite. The finished job restores the structural integrity of the pipe for decades of future use.

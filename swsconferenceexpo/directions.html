Hotel Information

HOLIDAY INN® TINLEY PARK

The Holiday Inn is adjacent to the Tinley Park Convention Center and features an enclosed walkway/entrance to the convention center. The lobby of this full-service hotel welcomes guests with its "home away from home" decor. Retire to one of the 202 comfortable guest rooms (10 suites) with amenities including a refrigerator, microwave, large work desk and 32-inch flat-screen TV. Guests have access to the 24-hour Business Center, complimentary Wi-Fi and free parking.

The group rate for the SWS Conference is $131/night. In order to receive this special rate, reservations must be made by Monday, October 22. Check-in time is 3:00 p.m. and check-out time is 11:00 a.m.
